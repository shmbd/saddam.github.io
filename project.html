Development of Probiotics for Human Health

Probiotics, defined as live microorganisms that, when administered in adequate amounts, confer a health benefit on the
host, have emerged as a promising approach for promoting human health and preventing or managing various diseases.
Traditionally associated with fermented foods and gut health, probiotics are now being explored for their broader
therapeutic potential, including immune modulation, metabolic regulation, and protection against pathogenic microbes.
